About

RGO Review Center Davao, established in 2016, is the Philippines' best and multi-awarded review center for Psychology with over 30 branches nationwide. We are well-known for producing multiple top-notchers on the Psychometrician Licensure Examinations. Our commitment and strategies to help you achieve your goals are always tailored to your learning needs.

Vision, Mission, and Goals

RGO Review Center Davao aims to put Filipino professionals on the global pedestal through our premier professional licensure review programs, drawing on our 19 years of experience in cumulative review for Psychometricians. The creation of the company stems from a desire to address potential professional disadvantages brought about by the pandemic in the tertiary academic system. RGO Review Center Davao aims to provide a specialist review service with a carefully selected core group of lecturers of the highest qualification and caliber. These individuals possess both theoretical strengths and practical experiences to share with Filipino test-takers, preparing them to become globally competitive in their fields.

How many slots are left and when is the deadline for enrollment?

Our available slots for each branch change every day as there are students enrolling on a day-to-day basis. Please send us a message if there are any slots left for you to choose. Once we reach a certain number, we may open a new section to accommodate the next batch. There is no deadline for enrollment as long as slots are available.

What are the inclusions in your review program?

When you enroll with us, you will receive 1 review kit with 7 books. In every review session, you will also answer drills, mock exams, and pre-boards. Over 5,000 actual board exam questions will be discussed. You will also receive guidance on how to apply for the PRC. Final coaching is also included. Motivation sessions and stress debriefing are integral parts of our review program.

Can I transfer to any of your branches if I miss a class in my enrolled branch?

Yes! We have an interbranch agreement for Mindanao. applicable to the following branches: Davao-CDO-Koronadal. That means that if you are enrolled in any of the mentioned branches, you can attend the class in any of these venues without additional cost. For example, if you are enrolled in Koronadal but you want to attend some of the sessions in Davao, yes you can do that and vice versa, just inform us.

Is it advisable to review even if there is no schedule for the exam yet?

Yes! We highly recommend that you enroll in our review program to keep your learning momentum and to prepare you for the big day. You lose nothing but gain more by continuing your studies after graduation. If the exam is moved to next year, students enrolled in this program will have the option to enroll in our next program at a discounted rate, or be given free online enhancement sessions.

I want to top or pass the board exam. What do I need to do?

You can enroll in our review program and follow our study guide. Our review program stood the test of time. Even during the pandemic, we were able to conduct the review and produce a high passing rate and top notchers.
